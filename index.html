About this VIT FFCS Planner

What is this VIT FFCS Planner?

This is a fast, no-login VIT FFCS planner built to help students test slot combinations instantly. Instead of going through multiple tabs or slow planners, you can directly click on slots and build your timetable in seconds.

How to use the FFCS Planner

  • Tap any cell in the timetable
  • Select a slot combination
  • Add subject names if needed
  • Avoid clashes automatically

You can quickly experiment with different FFCS slot combinations and finalize your timetable without confusion.

Why this planner is faster

  • No login or account required
  • Instant slot selection with visual feedback
  • Built specifically for VIT FFCS structure
  • Works smoothly on mobile and desktop

Who is this for?

This FFCS planner is designed for VIT students who want a quick and simple way to build their timetable during course registration. It is especially useful when testing multiple slot combinations under time pressure.
